9.2.3 Properties
Physical properties
Alkanes are almost non-polar molecules because of the covalent nature of C-C and
C-H bonds and due to very little difference of electronegativity between carbon and hydrogen atoms. They possess weak van der
Waals forces. Due to the weak forces, the first four members, C 1 to C 4 are gases, C 5 to C 17 are liquids and those containing 18 carbon atoms or more are solids at 298 K.
