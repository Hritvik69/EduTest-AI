ABOUT THE POET
Arun Kolatkar (1932–2004) is a contemporary Indian poet.
He was educated in Pune and earned a diploma in painting from the J.J. School of arts, Mumbai. He writes both in
English and Marathi and has authored two books. The present poem is an excerpt from Jejuri— a long poem in thirty-one sections. A German translation of Jejuri by
Gievanen Bandin was published in 1984.
